Classic Spaghetti with Tomato Marinara Sauce

Spaghetti is a classic Italian dish known for its long, thin pasta strands, typically made from durum wheat. It's often served with a variety of sauces, the most popular being marinara, a tomato-based sauce seasoned with herbs like basil and oregano. Spaghetti can also be topped with meatballs, Bolognese (a meat-based sauce), or simply garlic and olive oil. It's a versatile dish that's beloved worldwide for its simplicity and comforting nature.

  4. Serve: Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves.
