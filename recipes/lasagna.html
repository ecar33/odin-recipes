Classic Italian Lasagna with Bolognese Sauce

  1. Prepare Bolognese Sauce: Cook ground beef, onion, and garlic in a pan. Add tomato sauce, basil, and oregano. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare Cheese Mixture: In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, and an egg.
  3. Cook Lasagna Noodles: Boil lasagna noodles as per package instructions, then drain.
  4. Layer Lasagna: In a baking dish, layer Bolognese sauce, noodles, cheese mixture, and repeat. Top with mozzarella cheese.
  5. Bake: Cover with foil and bake at 375Â°F for 25 minutes. Uncover and bake for another 25 minutes.
  6. Rest and Serve: Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving.
